# Cover Image Prompt

Create a modern, professional wide-landscape book cover, 1200×630 pixels
(1.91:1 aspect ratio), for an educational book titled "Learning Graphs."

The image has three components:

1. A background montage of all the elements of a learning graph.  See the list below.
2. The title "Leaning Graph" in the center on top of the rest of the montage in white
3. The welcome image of the owl mascot in the lower left corner

TITLE TREATMENT:
The main title "Learning Graphs" appears in large, crisp, high-contrast
white sans-serif lettering, centered in the upper-middle of the image.
Directly beneath it, a smaller subtitle reads "The Key to Intelligent
Textbooks"
The title is bold and immediately readable.

BACKGROUND Montage Elements:

1. A sophisticated, softly glowing concept dependency graph — a directed
acyclic graph (DAG) — spanning the full width of the cover. Show luminous
circular nodes connected by thin directed edges (lines with subtle
arrowheads) that flow generally left to right, evoking a prerequisite
roadmap of ideas building on one another. Cluster some nodes into loose
groups tinted with different accent colors (teal, gold/amber, coral) to
suggest distinct knowledge domains, while other nodes stay deep blue.
Keep the network elegant and uncluttered: brighter, in-focus nodes behind
the title; a gentle depth-of-field haze toward the edges.

2. A rainbow colored Bloom Taxonomy triangle with the title "Bloom" above it

3. Students in a open-format classroom with computers on their desktops

COLOR PALETTE:
Deep academic navy-to-teal gradient background (#0d47a1 into teal),
luminous white title text, with warm gold/amber and coral node highlights.
Clean, modern, high-tech-education aesthetic.

MASCOT (place in the LOWER-LEFT CORNER):
A friendly academic owl mascot named Axiom in a welcoming pose. Compact
rounded body with deep academic blue feathers (#1976d2), a cream/ivory
face and chest, large golden-yellow eyes framed by dark brow markings that
resemble scholarly glasses, a warm amber-orange beak and talons, and a
small gold collar with a bright red tie. Both wings are open outward in a
gentle welcoming arc, upright posture with a slight forward lean, bright
friendly eyes, and a warm confident smile. Render Axiom in a clean
vector-style illustration with soft shading and flat colors. Give him a
soft subtle glow (or a slightly darker vignette behind him) so his blue
body stands out clearly and does not blend into the blue graph background.
Axiom sits in the front foreground plane, roughly one-quarter to one-third
the cover height, anchored in the lower-left and NOT overlapping the title.

COMPOSITION:
Title centered and dominant; DAG network fills the background; Axiom
anchored in the lower-left foreground with wings spread in welcome.
Balanced and tasteful, not cluttered. No additional characters, no logos,
no watermarks, no trademarks, and no text other than the title and subtitle.

STYLE:
Modern textbook cover, professional, educational, high-quality digital
illustration.
